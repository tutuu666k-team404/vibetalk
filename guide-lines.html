Guidelines - VibeTalk

Respectful Behavior

  • Treat others with respect, avoiding offensive or discriminatory language.
  • We do not tolerate bullying or harassment of any kind.
  • Avoid making offensive or provocative comments.
  • Do not create unnecessary conflicts, respect the opinions of others.
  • Do not spam or post unsolicited advertisements.
  • Avoid political or religious discussions that may cause conflicts.

Allowed Content

  • Share only appropriate and legal content.
  • It is not allowed to upload offensive, violent, or pornographic content.
  • Content must not violate third-party copyrights or intellectual property.
  • Images and videos must comply with privacy regulations and image rights.
  • Do not post misleading or fraudulent content.
  • Ensure that shared links are safe and do not lead to harmful sites.

Privacy and Security

  • Do not share sensitive personal information such as phone numbers, email addresses, or bank details.
  • Respect others' privacy, do not take screenshots or recordings without their consent.
  • Do not use the site for phishing, scams, or fraudulent activities.
  • If you suspect illegal behavior, report it immediately to the administrators.
  • Do not use the site to collect personal information from other users.

Interaction and Behavior in Chats

  • Do not send hate or violent messages.
  • Use appropriate and non-offensive language towards other users.
  • Avoid using language or expressions that incite hatred or violence.
  • Do not harass other users with repeated or intrusive messages.
  • Do not excessively use capital letters or special characters to attract attention in a disruptive manner.

Role of Administrators and Moderators

  • Administrators and moderators have the right to remove content, suspend, or ban users who violate the rules.
  • It is not allowed to contact administrators or moderators in a persistent or offensive manner.
  • If there is an issue, report it through the support system.

Abuse of Features

  • Do not abuse chat features to disturb or prevent others from using the service.
  • Do not use chat to automate spam or disruptive behaviors (e.g., bots).
  • Do not send links to content or software that could harm other users' systems.

Violations and Sanctions

  • Violating these guidelines may result in suspension or expulsion from the site.
  • In case of a severe violation, the user's account will be suspended permanently without warning.
  • All users are required to respect these rules, even if not explicitly stated in every case.

Rules for Using Mini-Games and Interactive Content

  • Make sure to follow the specific rules for each mini-game or contest.
  • Do not exploit bugs or glitches in games to gain unfair advantages.
  • Respect the behavior of other participants during interactive activities.
